GAA Football

Our GAA team has been successful at times over the years, winning the All-Ireland competiton in both 1992 and 2012.
We continously compete each year with either great or little to no success.

Other Sports

We also have many other sports like hurling, rugby, basketball and many many more. However none are to the same scale as GAA Football is in Donegal.




Music

Donegal is also very famous for our musicians, we have many bands originating in Donegal but also many famous single artists.
Artists like Rory Gallagher and Daniel O Donnell would go on to tour the world with their skillfull performances and topping charts whilst they do so.
